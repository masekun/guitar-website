About Our Approach

Founded by Kobe, our academy brings a unique fusion of electronic and string guitar techniques that create atmospheric, emotional experiences through music.

Our philosophy centers on developing your individual style while building a solid technical foundation. We believe in creating musicians, not just guitar players.
